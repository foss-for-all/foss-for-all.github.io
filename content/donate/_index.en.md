Please support FOSS for All — an initiative to build a sustainable Free and Open Source software ecosystem.

Free and open source software runs across our daily lives and industry, made possible by the many people who build and maintain it behind the scenes. FOSS for All operates so that the FOSS ecosystem in Korea can grow sustainably, and so that communities and projects can focus on what matters most in their work.

## Support us with ₩10,000 per month

FOSS for All is funded through monthly recurring donations of ₩10,000 (KRW). Please use Donation Box if you have a domestic Korean payment method, or GitHub Sponsors if you only have an international payment method.

Setting up the ₩10,000 monthly recurring donation automatically registers you as a **supporting member** of the organization, with no additional steps required. Supporting member status is automatically removed the month after your donation stops. See the [Become a member](/en/about/join/) page for details on membership.

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I receive a tax-deductible donation receipt?

Not at this time. The organization has not yet been designated as a public interest corporation under the Inheritance and Gift Tax Act, so we cannot issue tax-deductible donation receipts. As described in our [long-term roadmap](/en/about/#organizations-long-term-roadmap), we plan to pursue this designation after establishing a non-profit corporation, at which point receipt issuance would become possible.

### Can I make a one-time donation?

Currently we only operate recurring donations. Donation Box recurring donations can be canceled immediately after a single payment with no issues, so you can use it like a one-time donation if you prefer.

### I'd like to join the organization as a member

If you'd like to participate more actively, please consider becoming a full member. See the [Become a member](/en/about/join/) page for details.
